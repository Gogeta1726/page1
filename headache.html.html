MEDICATION REQUIRED

Remedies that may reduce headache pain include aspirin, paracetamol and ibuprofen. Resting in a darkened room may also help.
Drink water. Inadequate hydration may lead you to develop a headache. ...
Get adequate sleep.
Soothe pain with a cold compress.
Drink caffeinated tea or coffee.
Relax with yoga.
Avoid strong smells.

SUGGESTIONS

If you’re looking for ways to prevent headaches, the following tips may help.
Stay hydrated.
Eat regularly.
Get the right amount of sleep.
Try to manage your stress.

SEEKING MEDICAL CARE

See a doctor immediately if you:
Feel worse than usual.
Become confused, slur your speech or faint.
Have one-sided numbness or paralysis, or trouble seeing, speaking or walking.
Develop a fever higher than 102°F.
Experience nausea or vomiting.
